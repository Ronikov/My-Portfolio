ABOUT ME

Hey! I'm Zulfami — a game design student who fell in love with games long before I understood how they were made. Now I'm obsessed with why certain mechanics feel amazing and how a single level can tell an entire story.

I specialise in game feel, level design, and narrative systems. Whether it's a cozy pixel platformer or a tightly-tuned puzzle, I want every moment to feel intentional and fun.
